Your data, your call

Health data this personal should never be the product.

Steady is built privacy-first, because the fastest way to lose your trust is to sell it. Your numbers are yours. We make money when the app is worth paying for, never by moving your data.

  • Encrypted end to endYour data is encrypted in transit and at rest, on infrastructure built to HIPAA standards.
  • Never sold, never adsWe will never sell or rent your health data, and Steady will never show you an ad.
  • Yours to take or deleteExport everything or delete your account for good, any time, in a couple of taps.

A simple promise

You should be able to manage a serious condition without signing your most private information away. Steady is designed so you never have to choose between help and privacy.
